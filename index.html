Yes List Page — Wireframe Review Guide

This guide is for internal stakeholders reviewing the mobile wireframe for the BCPP Yes List page. Use it to understand content, content structure, and interactive behaviors.

What's in this wireframe
① Yes List main page (mobile, 375px)
② Product results child page (grid + list views)
③ Trust pillar detail drawers (3 sections)
④ Filter panel, active filter tags, sort

Wireframe Notes & Conventions

  • All colors are intentionally grayscale — this is a wireframe, not a visual design
  • Gray boxes labeled "Image" are placeholders for actual photos or logos
  • Brand names (Brand A, Brand B, etc.) are placeholders for real brand partners; up to 60 at launch
  • Product data (names, prices, types) is sample data simulating the real database structure
  • Width is fixed at 375px (standard mobile) for review purposes
  • [SMALLER LOGO placeholder] in the hero is a layout note, not user-facing text

Category Browse

9 category icons scroll horizontally. Each is clickable and navigates to the product results page filtered by that category. Categories: Makeup, Nails, Face & Body, Oral Care, Hair, Fragrance, Babies & Kids, Men, Sun.

Search Bar

Clicking the 🔍 button navigates to the product results child page. The search bar does not expand inline.

Brand Grid

Starts with 9 placeholder brand cards; up to 60 brands at launch. Each card is fully clickable → product results filtered to that brand. "View more brands" expands the grid by 9 cards at a time. Final brands and logos TBD.

Trust Pillar Cards

Three stacked cards near the bottom: Our Screening Criteria, Proven Science & Advocacy, Top Medical & Scientific Experts. Each card is tappable and opens a bottom drawer with the full section content from the Proof Points document. No external CTA links in the drawers.

Email Signup

Standard newsletter signup. Fields: First Name, Last Name, Email. reCAPTCHA disclaimer included.

Product Results Page — Behaviors

Navigation

Users arrive here from: (a) brand card tap, (b) category icon tap, or (c) search bar tap.

Filters Available

  • Brand — single select from available brands
  • Product Type — multi-select (Cleanser, Moisturizer, Sunscreen, etc.)
  • Price Range — Under $15 / $15–$25 / $25–$40 / $40+

Active filters appear as dismissible tags below the filter bar. "Clear all" removes all filters.

View Toggle

Users can switch between Grid view (2-column, image + name + price + Buy button) and List view (single column, image thumbnail + more detail). Both views are live in this wireframe.

Sort Options

A–Z, Z–A, Price: Low–High, Price: High–Low. Functional in the wireframe.

"Buy" Buttons

Each product's Buy ↗ button navigates directly to that product's page on the retailer's website (opens in new tab). A placeholder retailer product page is included in this wireframe.
